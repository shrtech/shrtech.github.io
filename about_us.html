Little About Me

Reya, a daughter, a journey and relentless passion to bring unconventional south Asian, Indian and Persian inspired ice-cream flavors in the north west region. We make unique, vegetarian and no alcohol products with 100% locally sourced milk and cream. We make all of our products in Washington State, supporting other local businesses and partners.

Our journey began with a deep love for frozen desserts, South Asian cuisine, its spices, culture, and way of life. We were inspired to share this passion with others in the Northwest region, driven by our desire to introduce authentic South Asian, Persian, and Indian flavors into our handcrafted ice creams and other products. Our 4 year old daughter, Reya, played a pivotal role in motivating us to embark on this journey.

We aspire to create many more sweet and cherished memories for you and your family through our products, Namaste.

We strive to be a triple bottom line company, who focuses on bringing diverse culture, sustainability, eco-friendliness and organic business growth.
